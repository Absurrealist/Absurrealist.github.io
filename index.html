This application will guide you through the FOI request process. Please ensure you have adequate time to complete this journey. Sessions may expire. Forms may change. Progress is not guaranteed.

The Freedom of Information Act 1982 (Cth) provides citizens with the right to access government-held information, subject to certain exemptions and conditions as determined by the Department (or as may be determined). This portal facilitates the submission and processing of FOI requests in accordance with legislative requirements and administrative protocols (which may or may not apply).

FAQ: Questions That May Be Frequently Asked

Q: How long does the FOI process take?
A: Processing times vary depending on complexity, departmental capacity, staffing levels, bureaucratic impediments, and factors beyond our control or knowledge.

Q: What information can I request?
A: You may request any information that may or may not be held by the Department, subject to exemptions (which are numerous and subject to interpretation).

Q: What if my request is denied?
A: You may appeal the decision through appropriate channels as outlined in documentation that will or will not be provided.

Q: Who can submit an FOI request?
A: Any person or entity may submit a request, provided they meet eligibility criteria established by the Department (criteria subject to change without notice).
